About Me

I'm Ayeza Ali — a cybersecurity graduate from Dawood University of Engineering & Technology, Karachi. My academic journey gave me a strong theoretical foundation in digital security, and I'm now actively building hands-on experience through real-world projects and research.

During my studies, I worked with a range of programming languages and security concepts — from C++ and Python to web technologies like JavaScript and PHP. I approach each new technology with curiosity and a systematic mindset, committed to understanding not just the "how" but the "why."

> "Combining technical knowledge, analytical thinking,

Outside of pure security work, I've gained valuable professional experience as a Project Coordinator in the animation industry, managing 2D and 3D production workflows and keeping cross-functional teams on schedule. I've also worked in sales, which gave me strong client communication and interpersonal skills.

This combination of technical depth and professional experience makes me a well-rounded candidate — someone who can not only analyze systems but also clearly communicate findings, coordinate with teams, and deliver results.
